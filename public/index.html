Downtime Reporting Module

Used to find out instantaneous info about any machine or individual downtime.

Downtime reporting is a technique that allows reliability engineers to understand equipment and how it is failing.

Downtime analysis (DTA) is an essential part of plant operations management as it provides a powerful tool which enables a better understanding of the underlying issues that affect plant availability and rate loss.

Maintenance Application Module

Maintenance Application Module (MAM) is a mechanized claims processing and information retrieval system.

MAM refers to the complementary networks of hardware and software cooperating to collect, process, store, and disseminate information in order to support the managerial role of leveraging information technology to increase business value and profits.

Maintenance Application Module can be used in that context for decision making, coordination, control, analysis, and visualization.Maintenance Application Module as an academic discipline studies people, technology, organizations, and the relationships among them.
